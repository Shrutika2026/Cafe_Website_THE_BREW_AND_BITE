Our Heritage

The Brew & Bite Story

We Serve Passion, Brew Quality, & Bake Joy Since 2015

The Brew & Bite Café was founded on a simple philosophy: to bring together premium single-origin coffee beans and delicious, fresh gourmet bites in a beautiful, warm, and creative ambiance. Our beans are sourced directly from ethical, sustainable farms in Coorg and roasted locally to extract peak flavor profile.
